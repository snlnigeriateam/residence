Residence Rules
  1. Check-In/Check-Out: Check-In is at 1:00pm while Check-Out is at 10am. Please respect these times to allow for a smooth transition
  2. Respect for Property: Treat the Residence as you would your own home. Please report any accidental damages immediately to our Team
  3. No Smoking: The Residence is a non-smoking property.
  4. Pets: No pets are allowed on the premises.
  5. Maximum Occupancy: Guests are limited to the number agreed upon at the time of the Booking.
  6. Security: The Residence provides an on-premises security staff. Please ensure that all doors and windows are securely shut before checking out.
  7. Applicances and Utilities: Use appliances and utilities responsibly. Turn off lights, air-conditioning and heating when not in use.
  8. Waste Disposal: Garbage collection bins are provided on the premises. Please dispose of all waste properly.


Failure to comply with these rules may result in additional charges or eviction from the property. We appreciate your cooperation and hope you enjoy your stay at the Residence.
